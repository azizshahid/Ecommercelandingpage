FAQ

How do I place an order?
Simply browse our categories, select your desired items, and click "Add to Cart." Once you're ready, proceed to checkout and follow the instructions to complete your purchase.



What are the shipping options?
We offer standard and express shipping options. Shipping rates and times vary by location and will be calculated at checkout.



Can I return or exchange an item?
Yes, we have a 30-day return policy for eligible items. Visit our Return Policy page for details.



What payment methods do you accept?
We accept various payment methods, including major credit and debit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment options. You can see the available payment options during checkout.



How can I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on our website or the carrier's tracking page.



How do I become a seller on CraftyMarket?
We're always excited to welcome new artisans! Click "Become a Seller" on our homepage to start your application process.



Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we ship to many countries worldwide. Shipping fees and delivery times will be calculated at checkout based on your location. Customs fees may apply and are the buyer's responsibility.
